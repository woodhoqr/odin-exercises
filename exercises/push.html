Muscles Worked in Pushing Motions

Pushing motions work what would be considered muscles on the anterior of the body, with secondary muscles on the posterior worked as well.

What muscles are worked in a push up

  • Pectorals
  • Deltoids
  • Triceps
  • Core

When planning out a "push day", I have found it best to order pushing movements based on orientation, supersetted each with a core exercise. This gives about 4 push exercises and 2-4 core exercises. You can add 2 accessory exercises for the smaller muscle worked, as well! This is completely optional, however

Sample Push Day

  1. Horizontal Push: Push Ups
  2. Vertical Push: Overhead Press
  3. Core: Dead bug
  4. Horizontal Push: Flat Bench Press
  5. Vertical Push: Arnold Press
  6. Core: Cable Crunch
  7. Accessory (Optional): Triceps Dip
  8. Accessory (Optional): Triceps Push down
