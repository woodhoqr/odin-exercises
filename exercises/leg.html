Legs

Muscles Worked in a Compound Leg Movement

Leg day is used to work your entire leg. Front and back, top and bottom. We don't want to be able to walk straight after an amazing leg day!!!

What muscles are worked in a squat

  • Quads
  • Glutes
  • Calves
  • Hamstrings
  • Core

When planning out a "leg day", I have found it best to order pushing movements based on whether it's the front or the back (quad or hamstring dominanat) of the leg, supersetted each with a core exercise. This gives about 4 leg exercises and 2-4 core exercises. You can add 2 accessory exercises for the smaller muscles worked, as well! This is optional!

Sample Leg Day

  1. Quad Dom Leg: Back Squat
  2. Hamstring Dominant Leg: Lunges
  3. Core: Palloff Press
  4. Quad Dom Leg: Step Ups
  5. Hamstring Dominant Leg: Hip Thrusts
  6. Core: Swiss Ball Walkouts
  7. Accessory (Optional): Calf Raise
  8. Accessory (Optional): Seated Calf Raise
